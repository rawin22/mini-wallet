Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about WinPay

Getting Started

What is WinPay?

WinPay is a digital wallet and payment platform that allows you to:

  • Hold multiple currencies in one account
  • Send instant payments to other WinPay users
  • Make international wire transfers
  • Exchange currencies at competitive rates
  • Withdraw cash at Cash Points worldwide
How do I create an account?

To create a WinPay account:

  • Visit our website or download the app
  • Click "Sign Up" and enter your email
  • Complete identity verification (KYC)
  • Set up your security credentials
  • Your account will be ready within 24 hours
What is my WPAY_ID?

Your WPAY_ID is your unique identifier on the WinPay platform. It's used for:

  • Receiving instant payments from other users
  • Depositing cash at Cash Points (shown as QR code)
  • Referencing your wire deposits

You can find your WPAY_ID in the app under Profile or on the Deposit page.

Deposits & Withdrawals

How do I deposit funds?

There are two ways to deposit funds:

  • Cash Deposit: Visit a Cash Point, show your WPAY_ID QR code, and the agent will send funds to your account instantly.
  • Wire Transfer: Transfer funds from your bank to WinPay's bank account. Include your WPAY_ID as the reference. Funds credit within 1-3 business days.
How do I withdraw cash?

To withdraw cash:

  • Go to the "Withdraw" section in the app
  • Select your country/location
  • Choose a nearby Cash Point
  • Enter the amount and confirm
  • Visit the Cash Point with your reference number
  • Show your ID and collect your cash
What are the deposit limits?

Deposit limits depend on your verification level:

  • Basic: $1,000/day, $5,000/month
  • Verified: $10,000/day, $50,000/month
  • Premium: $100,000/day, unlimited monthly

Contact support to increase your limits.

Payments & Transfers

How do I send money to another user?

To send an instant payment:

  • Go to "Pay Now" in the app
  • Enter the recipient's WPAY_ID
  • Select currency and enter amount
  • Add an optional memo
  • Confirm and the payment is sent instantly!
How do I make an international wire transfer?

Use the Payment Wizard for international wires:

  • Select the destination country
  • Choose the currency and enter amount
  • Select or add a beneficiary
  • Review the FX quote and book the rate
  • Submit the payment

Wire transfers typically arrive within 1-3 business days.

What are the transfer fees?

Our fee structure:

  • Instant Payments (P2P): Free
  • Cash Deposits: 1% (min $1)
  • Cash Withdrawals: 1.5% (min $2)
  • Wire Transfers: $15-25 depending on destination
  • Currency Exchange: 0.5% spread

Security

Is WinPay safe to use?

Yes! WinPay employs multiple security measures:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all data
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA)
  • Biometric login (fingerprint/Face ID)
  • Licensed and regulated by MAS (Singapore)
  • Funds held in segregated client accounts
What should I do if I suspect fraud?

If you suspect unauthorized activity:

  • Immediately lock your account from the app
  • Contact our emergency line: +65 6123 4569
  • Do not share your login credentials
  • Report the incident to our compliance team
